Bundle & Package FAQs

Do I have to choose the suggested bundles?

No. You can create any bundle you want by choosing 3+ services. The 15% discount applies to any combination. Tell us what you need and we'll build a custom package for you.

What if I want different services on different days?

We can schedule different services on different dates, and you still get the 15% discount on the total if it's 3+ services. We'll work around your schedule.

Can I add services later and still get the discount?

Yes. Start with a bundle and add more services within 30 days—the 15% discount applies to the new services too if your total is 3+.

Your free estimate shows each service's cost, the 15% discount applied, and the final total. No hidden fees, no surprise charges. You'll know exactly what to expect.
