What We Offer

EXTERIOR CLEANING SERVICES

Pressure Washing

Driveways · Patios · Concrete · Trash Bins

  • High-pressure water blasts away years of embedded dirt, oil stains, and grime from hard surfaces that nothing else can touch.
  • Dirty driveways and patios drag down your curb appeal — a single pressure wash can make them look new again.
  • Regular cleaning prevents buildup that etches and degrades concrete, pavers, and wood over time.

Soft Washing

House Siding · Roofs · Stucco · Wood · Fences · Painted Surfaces

  • Low-pressure, biodegradable solution kills mold, mildew, and algae at the root — safe for paint, stucco, and delicate surfaces pressure washing would damage.
  • San Diego's coastal humidity feeds biological growth year-round. Soft washing stops it before it spreads and causes permanent staining or rot.
  • Extends the life of your siding and roof by years, pushing off expensive repaints and refinishing jobs.

Solar Panel Cleaning

Residential · Commercial · All Panel Types

  • Dirty panels lose 15–30% of their energy output — regular cleaning pays for itself in recovered production.
  • We use a RO/DI water filtration system and water-fed pole to deliver pure, spot-free water — nothing is left behind when it dries.
  • Bird droppings cause hot spots that permanently degrade cells. Cleaning prevents damage, not just dirt.

Window & Screen Cleaning

Exterior · Screens · Tracks

  • Hard water and salt air leave mineral deposits that etch into glass permanently — regular cleaning prevents irreversible damage.
  • We use a RO/DI water filter and water-fed pole system — purified water leaves zero minerals or residue on the glass when it evaporates, so you get a truly spot-free finish.
  • Clean windows let in measurably more light, improving the feel of every room without changing anything inside.

Gutter Cleaning

Flush · Inspect · Clear Downspouts

  • Clogged gutters are a leading cause of foundation and roof damage — water backs up and works its way in over time.
  • San Diego's dry season lulls homeowners into skipping checks — then the first heavy rain overwhelms a year's worth of debris.
  • One of the cheapest preventive maintenance tasks available, and one of the most expensive to ignore.

Home Reset

Bundle & Save · Full Exterior Refresh

  • Pair 3 or more services and get 15% off your entire order — the more you bundle, the more you save.
  • Give your home a complete exterior reset in a single visit — no juggling multiple appointments or service providers.
  • Popular combos: Soft Wash + Windows + Gutters · Pressure Wash + Solar + Gutters · Full home packages available.
