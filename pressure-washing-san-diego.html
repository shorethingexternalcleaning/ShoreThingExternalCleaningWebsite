Why Pressure Washing Matters in San Diego

San Diego's coastal climate, combined with year-round sunshine, creates ideal conditions for dirt, algae, salt residue, and oil stains to accumulate on your property's exterior surfaces. Over time, these buildups don't just look bad—they damage concrete, pavers, wood, and driveways by etching into the surface and accelerating deterioration.

Pressure washing removes embedded contaminants that regular cleaning cannot touch, restoring surfaces to near-original condition in a single visit. A clean driveway, patio, or parking lot instantly improves curb appeal, increases property value, and prevents expensive restoration work years down the line.

Pressure Washing FAQs

Is pressure washing safe for all surfaces?

Professional pressure washing is safe when done correctly. We adjust water pressure based on the surface type—lower pressure for delicate areas like stucco and higher pressure for concrete. Our technicians know exactly what PSI and technique each surface needs. Improper DIY pressure washing can damage wood, paint, and roofing, which is why professionals are worth the investment.

How often should I pressure wash my driveway?

Most San Diego homeowners benefit from pressure washing their driveway once or twice per year. Driveways in high-traffic areas or with heavy oil stains may need more frequent service. Gutters and soft-wash surfaces need cleaning every 1–2 years depending on environmental factors. We can assess your property and recommend a schedule.

What's the difference between pressure washing and soft washing?

Pressure washing uses high-pressure water (1500+ PSI) to remove tough grime from hard surfaces like concrete and pavement. Soft washing uses low-pressure water mixed with biodegradable cleaning solutions to safely remove mold, algae, and stains from delicate surfaces like siding, roofs, and stucco. We offer both services tailored to your property.

Will pressure washing damage my landscaping or plants?

We take care to protect your landscaping during pressure washing. We'll relocate potted plants, shield delicate shrubs, and adjust water pressure away from garden areas. Our technicians are experienced working around yards and take preventive measures to keep your plants safe.

How much does pressure washing cost in San Diego?

Pricing depends on the size of the area, the type of surface, and the level of buildup. A typical driveway might range from $250–$500, while larger projects scale accordingly. We provide free, no-obligation estimates so you know the cost before work begins. Many customers save money by bundling pressure washing with other services (soft washing, window cleaning, etc.)
