Why Soft Washing Is Essential in San Diego

San Diego's coastal humidity and mild winters create perfect conditions for mold, mildew, algae, and lichen to thrive year-round. High-pressure washing can damage delicate surfaces like stucco, paint, wood, and roofing shingles. That's where soft washing excels: it uses low pressure combined with biodegradable, eco-friendly cleaning solutions to eliminate biological growth at the root, not just the surface.

Unlike pressure washing, soft washing prevents regrowth for 1–2 years or longer, making the long-term solution more cost-effective. Your home's exterior stays protected, preserved, and beautiful without compromising structural integrity or aesthetic value.

Soft Washing FAQs

What's the difference between soft washing and pressure washing?

Pressure washing uses high-pressure water (1500+ PSI) to clean hard surfaces like concrete. Soft washing uses low-pressure water (under 500 PSI) combined with eco-friendly cleaning solutions to safely clean and treat delicate surfaces like roofs, siding, and painted areas. Soft washing prevents regrowth of mold and algae, while pressure washing is better for heavy grime removal.

Is soft wash solution safe for my plants and pets?

Yes. We use biodegradable, EPA-approved cleaning solutions that are safe for gardens, plants, and pets when used according to our protocols. We take precautions to rinse landscaping thoroughly and protect sensitive areas. Your family and pets are safe during and after the cleaning.

How long do soft wash results last?

Soft wash results typically last 1–2 years, depending on climate and surface type. In San Diego's humid coastal areas, algae and mildew grow faster, so homeowners near the coast often schedule soft washing annually. Inland properties may need service every 18–24 months. We'll recommend a schedule based on your property's specific needs.

Can soft washing damage my roof or siding?

No. Soft washing is specifically designed to be gentle on delicate surfaces. We avoid the high pressures that can cause leaks, dent siding, or damage paint. Our training and equipment ensure thorough cleaning without damage—we treat your home like our own.

What's included in a soft wash service?

A typical soft wash service includes careful assessment, application of biodegradable cleaning solution, gentle low-pressure rinsing, and thorough coverage of all targeted areas. We also take steps to protect landscaping, windows, and other sensitive areas. You'll receive a free estimate before work begins.
